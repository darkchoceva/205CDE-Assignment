A Few Words About Us

We Voluntary Help for Almost 0 Years

weCare is one of the largest charity organizations connecting nonprofits, volunteers and donors. We provide voluntary help to many countries including Asian countries for almost 10 years. Also, we held a lot of charities and collect donation to make our world a better place.

For the past few years, we have done a few big service project such as provide clean water in Africa, help to educate the children in Asia, build houses for people who are homeless and provide suffiecient food to end world hunger. weCare will give our best to help the communities through their hard times so that they are loved and cared by the world.

Mission

weCare is a nonprofit oraganization showing love and support to people, improve basic knowledge of children, end world hunger and poverty. Kindness of yours is someone's hope.

Vision

Believes that we can end hunger & poverty, save the people who are homeless and without education. Ensuring the equality to everyone.
